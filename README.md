Sales and Profit Insights Dashboard 
 
📌Project Overview
The Sales and Profit Insights Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze sales performance, profit distribution, customer segments, and category-wise business growth.

This dashboard helps businesses monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify profitable categories, and understand sales trends for better decision-making.
🎯Problem Statement

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data daily, making it difficult to track performance and identify profitable areas efficiently.

The objective of this project was to transform raw sales data into a visually interactive dashboard that provides meaningful insights into sales, profit, discounts, customer segments, and category performance.

📈Key Insights
The business generated 2.30M total sales and 286.40K total profit.
The Technology category contributed the highest sales and profit among all categories.
Consumer segment customers generated the highest share of total sales.
Certain sub-categories such as Copiers and Phones produced higher profits.
Furniture category showed comparatively lower sales and profitability.
Sales trends indicate that product categories contribute differently to overall business growth.
